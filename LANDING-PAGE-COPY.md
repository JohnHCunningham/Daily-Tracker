#### Sandler Selling System
Focus on pain funnel, up-front contracts, and negative reverse selling. Get coaching on:
- Did you set an up-front contract?
- How deep did you go in the pain funnel?
- Talk/listen ratio (target: 30/70)
- Budget discussion before presentation

**Best for:** Consultative sales, high-touch B2B

#### MEDDIC/MEDDPICC
Qualify hard. Get coaching on:
- Metrics identified?
- Economic buyer confirmed?
- Decision criteria clear?
- Champion identified?

**Best for:** Enterprise sales, complex deals

#### Challenger Sale
Teach, tailor, take control. Get coaching on:
- Did you teach them something new?
- Did you tailor to their specific situation?
- Did you take control with constructive tension?

**Best for:** Disruptive solutions, status quo challengers

#### SPIN Selling
Question-based discovery. Get coaching on:
- Situation questions asked?
- Problem questions to uncover pain?
- Implication questions to build urgency?
- Need-payoff questions to create value?

**Best for:** Solution selling, consultative approaches

#### Gap Selling
Problem-centric selling. Get coaching on:
- Current state analysis depth?
- Future state vision created?
- Gap explicitly stated?
- Cost of inaction discussed?

**Best for:** Problem-solving sales, transformation selling

## ‚ùì FAQ

### Headline
**Frequently Asked Questions**

#### Q: Does this work with my sales methodology?
**A:** Yes! We support MEDDIC, Sandler, Challenger, SPIN, and Gap Selling. You can also use general sales best practices if you don't follow a specific methodology. The AI adapts its coaching to your framework.

#### Q: Do I need to record my calls?
**A:** No. You can paste transcripts from Zoom, Gong, Chorus, or any other tool. If you do have audio recordings, we can transcribe them (coming soon).

#### Q: How long does analysis take?
**A:** Under 30 seconds. Upload a transcript, click "Analyze," and get instant coaching with tactical scripts.

#### Q: Can I customize the scripts?
**A:** Absolutely. Describe your business and we'll generate custom opening scripts, value props, CTAs, and objection handlers. Edit them to match your exact wording. The AI will compare your actual calls to YOUR scripts.

#### Q: Is my data secure?
**A:** Yes. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256). Your conversations are stored securely on Supabase with Row Level Security. We never share your data with third parties.

#### Q: Can managers see their team's performance?
**A:** Yes. The Team and Enterprise plans include an Admin Dashboard showing team-wide metrics: activity levels, close rates, methodology execution scores, and client engagement.

#### Q: What if I want to white-label this for my clients?
**A:** Our Enterprise plan includes white-label branding. Use your logo, colors, and domain. Perfect for consultants and agencies reselling to clients.

#### Q: Do you offer training on the methodologies?
**A:** The platform coaches on methodology execution, but we assume your team already knows the framework (Sandler, MEDDIC, etc.). For Enterprise customers, we can connect you with certified trainers.

#### Q: Can I cancel anytime?
**A:** Yes. No contracts. Cancel anytime. Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation.
